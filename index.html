Smart Daily Budget Tracker — ZakaWise™

Track it. Understand it. Master your money.

Purpose

Most people don’t know where their money goes daily. The ZakaWise app makes it simple and visual — especially for young professionals or students — to track spending habits and set mini budgets with ease.

What It Does

Helps users track daily expenses, categorize spending, and build smarter habits using visual tools, reminders, and practical suggestions.

Innovation Angle

  • Promotes financial literacy among youth and new account holders.
  • Encourages daily engagement with personal finances.
  • Can be integrated with bank accounts or card spend alerts in the future.
  • Potential to add gamification (e.g., "streaks" for staying under budget).
